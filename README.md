## About

Exhale helps you track your vaping habits with real data, not vibes. Pair it with the physical Exhale module, a small attachment for your device that logs your sessions automatically, no logging, no thinking, just vibing while it tracks everything in the background.

Log your sessions, watch your trends, and decide for yourself how deep the rabbit hole goes.

## How It Works

1. **Attach the module** to your vape device.
2. **Vape as usual.** The module tracks your sessions automatically, mindlessly, effortlessly.
3. **Open the app** to see your real numbers whenever you're ready for the red pill.
